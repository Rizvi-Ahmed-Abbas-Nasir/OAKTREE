Our Story

How It All Began

I have been helping people manage their money since 1995. Back then, my focus was on recommending the right financial products based on my clients' needs. In 2008, I took my passion further by becoming a Certified Financial Planner. That’s when Oaktree was born—a place where financial planning goes beyond numbers to create a clear, step-by-step path for life goals.

At Oaktree, I believe that having a PLAN and strategy gives people a sense of satisfaction and leads to a fulfilling life. With a well-thought-out plan, you can embrace every milestone with confidence and awareness.
